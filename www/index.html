Voice Commands Guide

You can speak naturally to describe a visit. Additionally, you can speak these quick commands to control the app instantly (offline):

Form Autofill Shortcuts:
  • "set name to [name]" (e.g. "set name to John Doe")
  • "set phone to [number]" (e.g. "set phone to 9876543210")
Action Controls:
  • "clear diagnosis" / "clear medicines"
  • "save record" (Saves the active form)
  • "close modal" / "cancel" (Closes modal)
Hindi / हिन्दी Equivalents:
  • "naam [name]" (e.g. "naam Rahul Kumar")
  • "phone [number]" (e.g. "phone 9988776655")
  • "save karo" / "clear karo"
